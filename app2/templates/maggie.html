Maggi Noodles

5 mins prep 2 mins cook 1 serving 4.7 (1.2k reviews)

Quick and delicious instant noodles that can be prepared in just 2 minutes with hot water. Perfect for students, busy professionals, or anyone craving a tasty snack.

Ingredients

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Boil 1.5 cups of water in a pan or kettle. For better flavor, you can use vegetable or chicken stock instead of plain water.

  2. Once the water is boiling, add the Maggi noodles and the included tastemaker. If you're adding vegetables, add them now.

  3. Cook for 2 minutes, stirring occasionally. If you prefer softer noodles, cook for an additional 30 seconds to 1 minute.

  4. Turn off the heat and add a teaspoon of butter or oil if desired. This adds richness to the noodles.

  5. Transfer to a bowl, garnish with chili flakes if using, and serve immediately. Enjoy your hot, delicious Maggi noodles!

Chef's Tips

  • For creamier noodles, add a splash of milk at the end of cooking.
  • Try adding an egg for extra protein - crack it into the boiling noodles and stir.
  • For a spicy kick, add a few drops of hot sauce or some chopped green chilies.
  • Leftover cooked chicken or shrimp makes a great addition for a more substantial meal.
