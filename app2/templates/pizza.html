Authentic Margherita Pizza

30 mins 2 servings 4.9 (2.4k reviews) Italian

The classic Neapolitan pizza featuring San Marzano tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, basil, and extra-virgin olive oil. Simple, fresh, and delicious!

Ingredients

Instructions

1

Prepare the Dough

Let the pizza dough rest at room temperature for 1-2 hours if refrigerated. Preheat your oven to 500°F (260°C) with a pizza stone inside for at least 30 minutes.

2

Shape the Pizza

On a floured surface, stretch the dough into a 12-inch circle. Transfer it to a pizza peel or parchment paper dusted with flour or cornmeal.

3

Add Toppings

Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a 1-inch border. Tear the mozzarella into small pieces and distribute it over the sauce. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with a pinch of sea salt.

4

Bake the Pizza

Slide the pizza onto the preheated stone and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.

5

Finish and Serve

Remove from the oven, top with fresh basil leaves, and drizzle with a little more olive oil. Slice and serve immediately.
